Privacy Policy

How AdShubh Lifeline handles your personal information.

Data Collection

We collect donor and request information including name, contact details, blood group, and location solely for blood donation coordination purposes.

Data Storage

In this platform version, data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

Verified donor profiles (name, blood group, city, availability) are visible to users searching for blood. Contact details are accessible only through direct contact actions.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting admin@adshubhlifeline.in or through the admin panel.
