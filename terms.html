Terms of Use

Terms governing use of the AdShubh Lifeline platform.

Platform Purpose

AdShubh Lifeline is a blood donation coordination platform. It facilitates connections between donors and recipients but does not provide medical services.

User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate information. False emergency requests or fraudulent registrations may result in account removal.

Verification

All donors and requests undergo admin verification. The platform does not guarantee immediate blood availability.

Limitation of Liability

AdShubh Lifeline is provided as-is. Users are responsible for verifying donor eligibility and medical suitability before donation.
